Status Report // April 2026

Intelligence in the Wild

Moving from generative toys to autonomous agents.

The world of AI in 2026 is less about the "magic" of a chatting box and more about the "machinery" of autonomous execution. We’ve moved past simple prompts into a world where AI systems handle end-to-end tasks.

The State of AI

Agentic Workflows

Moving from "Draft an email" to "Execute this project plan, coordinate with the team, and report back when finished."

Local Sovereignty

Enterprises are pulling away from massive public APIs in favor of highly optimized, locally-hosted models for privacy.

Inference Efficiency

New compression techniques allow billion-parameter models to run on mobile hardware with minimal latency.
